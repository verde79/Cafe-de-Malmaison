Welcome to Cafe de Malmaison

Malmaison is a newly refurbished restaurant situated in the old town of Fribourg, Switzerland. Inhabiting the old Café de Malmaison established in 1850s, the new Malmaison aims to serve a contemporary menu developed upon refined, classical cookinOur patrons are young professionals who enjoy a relaxed atmosphere to enjoy a meal with a partner of as a small group of friends.

Opening Times

Lunch, 12:00 - 15:00 During the afternoon, Café de Malmaison offers a light selection of pastries to be accompanies by our coffee prepared by our excellent barista as well as charcuterie and cheese selection, paired expertly with a selected range of wines hand picked by our sommelier.
